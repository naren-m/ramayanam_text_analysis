2::14::48::महीपतिः lord of the earth (the king), दैन्यात् due to the wretched conditon, स्वयम् personally, वक्तुम् to speak out, यदा when, न शशाक was not able, तदा then, मन्त्रज्ञा कैकेयी Kaikeyi, सुमन्त्रम् to Sumantra, प्रत्युवाच ह said in reply.
2::14::49::सुमन्त्र O Sumantra, राजा king, रामहर्षसमुत्सुकः excited with joy in connection with Rama's coronation, रजनीम् entire night, प्रजागरपरिश्रान्तः weary with keeping awake all night, निद्रावशम् overpowered with sleep, उपेयिवान् is affected.
2::14::50::तत् so, सूत O charioteer (Sumantra), त्वरितम् quickly, गच्छ go, यशस्विनम् illustrious, राजपुत्रम् prince, रामम् Rama, आनय bring, ते to you, भद्रम् gentle, अत्र in this matter, विचारणा hesitation, न कार्या need not be done.
2::14::51::सः he, हृदयेन at heart, कल्याणम् auspicious event, मन्यमानः thinking, ननन्द च felt happy, राजशासनात् at the royal command, त्वरितः swiftly, सम्प्रीत्याः rejoiced, निर्जगाम च went away.
2::14::52::तया by her, चोदितः urged, सुमन्त्रः Sumantra, चिन्तयामास thought over, धर्मवित् a knower of righteousness, रामः Rama, व्यक्तम् definitely, अभिषेकार्थम् for installation, इह here, आयास्यति  he will come.
2::14::53::सूतः the charioteer, इति in this way, मतिं कृत्वा having thought, महता हर्षेण with great delight, वृतः filled, महाबाहोः one with mighty arms, राघवस्य Rama's, दिदृक्षया  with an  intention to see Rama, निर्जगाम came out.
2::14::54::सुमन्त्रः Sumantra, सागरह्रदसङ्काशात् from a place which looked like deep waters of the sea, शुभात् auspicious, अन्तःपुरात् inner apartment, निष्क्रम्य having departed, अग्रतः ahead of him, जनसम्बाधम् thronged with people, द्वारम् gate, ददर्श saw.
2::14::55::ततः then, सहसा immediately, पुरस्तात् forward, विनिर्गतः moving, द्वारगतः having reached  the gate, विलोकयन् seeing, द्वारम् gate, उपेत्य having approached, विष्ठितान् waiting, महीपतीन् kings, उपस्थितान् having arrived, महाधनान् wealthy people, (विविधान्) पौरान् citizens, ददर्श beheld.
