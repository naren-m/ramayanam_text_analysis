2::80::3::हर्षात् with joy, तम् उद्देशम् to the appointed region, प्रयान् proceeding, सः that, विपुलः  multitude, जनौघः people, पर्वणि on fullmoon day, महावेगः with great speed, समुद्रः इव  like an ocean, अशोभत shone. 
2::80::4::वर्त्मकर्मणि in the construction of roads, कोविदाः skilled, ते those, विविधोपेतैः with different kinds of, करणैः tools, स्ववारम् their own group, समास्थाय taking up the place, पुरस्तात् ahead, सम्प्रतस्थिरे departed. 
2::80::5::लतावल्लीः च creepers, गुल्मांश्च shrubs, स्थाणून् branchless trees, अश्मन एव च boulders, विविधान् various, द्रुमान् trees, छिन्दन्त hewing off, मार्गम् way, जनायांचक्रिरे paved by the (king's) men.
2::80::6::केचित् some of them, अवृक्षेषु where there were no trees, देशेषु in places, वृक्षान् saplings, अरोपयन् planted, केचित् some, क्वचित् क्वचित् here and there, कुठारैः with axes, टङ्कैश्च with hatchets, दात्रैः च with sickles, छिन्दन् severing.
2::80::7::बलवत्तराः stronger men, अपरे some others, बलिनः strongrooted, वीरणस्तम्भान् tufts of fragrant grass, विधमन्ति स्म removed, ततस्ततः here and there, दुर्गाणि impassable terrains, स्थलानि were made even. 
2::80::8::अपरे some others, कूपान् wells, आयतम् wide, श्वभ्रम् chasm, पांसुभिः with loose soil, अपूरयन् filled, ततः thereafter, केचित् some, निम्नभागान् low level grounds, समन्ततः on all sides, समान् evenly, चक्रुः made. 
2::80::9::तदा then, नराः people, बन्धनीयान् required to be bridged, तांस्तान् at such, देशान् places, बबन्धु: bridged, क्षोद्यान् required crushing, सञ्चुक्षुदुः pulverised, भेदनीयान् च required to be split open, बिभिदुः broke them. 
2::80::10::अचिरेणकालेनैव in a short time, बहूदकान् having large volumes of water, बहुविधाकारान् of different shapes, सागरप्रतिमान् like the sea, बहून् many, परिवाहान् water courses, चक्रुः constructed. 
2::80::11::निर्जलेषु where there was no water, देशेषु in places, उत्तमान् best, बहुविधान् various, वेदिकापरिमण्डितान् surrounded by circular dykes, उदपानान् drinking water wells, खानयामासुः  were dug. 
2::80::12::ससुधाकुट्टिमतलः surface decorated with mosaic, प्रपुष्पितमहीरुहः avenue lined with blossoming trees, मत्तोद्घुष्ट द्विजगणः with flocks of birds intoxicated with singing, पताकाभिः with banners, अलङ्कृतः decorated, चन्दनोदकसंसिक्तः sprinkled with sandal water, नानाकुसुमभूषितः embellished with various kinds of flowers, सेनायाः army's सुरपथोपमः like the path of the gods, (सः) पन्थाः that highway, बहु brilliantly, अशोभत shone. 
2::80::13::अधिकृता authorised, ते नराः those persons, यथाज्ञप्ति in accordance with the orders (of Bharata), युक्ताः performing their work, आज्ञप्य issued orders (to the artisans), अथ thereafter, बहुस्वादुफलेषु with varieties of sweet fruits, रमणीयेषु lovely, देशेषु places, महात्मनः  of great, भरतस्य Bharata, यः निवेशः restingplace, अभिप्रेतः selected, तम् that place, भूषाभिः with ornaments, भूषणोपमम् like ornaments , भूयः well, शोभयामासुः shone.
2::80::14::तद्विदः men well versed in the study of planetary positions relating to auspicious constellations and auspicious time (astrologers), प्रशस्तेषु at auspicious, नक्षत्रेषु in the constellation, मुहूर्तेषु at auspicious time, महात्मनः highsouled, भरतस्य for Bharata, निवेशान् restplace, स्थापयामासुः  established. 
2::80::15::तत्र there, बहुपांसुचयाश्चपि heaps of sand, परिखापरिवारिताः encircled by moats, इन्द्रकीलप्रतिमाः comparable to mount Indrakeela, प्रतोलीवरशोभिताः adorned with broad highways, प्रासादमालावितताः lined with mansions, सौधप्राकारसंवृताः surrounded by walls, पताकाशोभिताः decorated with flags, सुनिर्मित महापथाः with wellbuilt, broad thoroughfares, ते निवेशाः those residences, आकाशे in the sky, विसर्पद्भिरिव like one spreading, समुच्छ्रितैः tall, विटङ्काग्रविमानकैः with the peaks of mansions, शक्रपुरोपमाः like the abode of Indra, बभुः shone.
